Barking Bath Dog Washing Tips

#1

Keep ear flaps down to prevent water from getting into ears.

#2

Hold your pet’s nose down while washing the face to help the water run off.

#3

Completely rinse all the shampoo off the dog… and don’t forget the underside!

#4

Wash your pet from rear to front. This will decrease your dog’s urge to shake.

#5

Be sure to wash the paws and don’t be shy.. wash the rump, too!
