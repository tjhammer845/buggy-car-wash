Welcome to Barking Bath Dog Wash

Keeping up with regular dog grooming can cost a small fortune and be a messy endeavor. Now you can treat your dog to a day at the spa without a big budget. Our two private rooms have all the equipment you need to make your dog’s coat shine. No more wet towels and hair in the bathtub and time consuming clean up, just come to the Barking Bath. All of the dogs are “barking” about it! For your convenience, we’re open 8am-5pm, weather permitting.
