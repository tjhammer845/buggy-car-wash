Welcome to Buggy Car Wash

Buggy Car Wash is a professional, locally owned car and dog wash in North Haven CT. Protect your vehicles’ finish from the elements with our advanced touch-free wash equipment, or try our soft touch car wash system. Our Ultimate Package will keep your car or truck spotless. We also have four self-service bays for those who like to get in there themselves. Also don’t miss our self-service dog wash rooms that are equipped with multiple shampoos, conditioner, dryers, and vacs.

Two Automatic Bays

A touch-free wash using high pressure water and soap or the soft-touch car wash with soft pads that gently wash the vehicle.

Self-Service Bays

Try our most economical wash. Our self-service bays are huge. Bring your car, truck, trailer or boat. Open 24 hours for your convenience.
